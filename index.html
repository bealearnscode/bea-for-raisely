Customer Advocacy Idol

I think Ally Bank has the best customer service I have experienced so far. They are available around the clock and their mobile and web apps tell you how long your wait will be before you even decide to make a call. They respond quickly via email and social media and their customer reps are great to talk to. I know some companies want their customer service reps to sound overly cheerful and it can be very off putting if that is not your natural demeanor. Ally's customer reps are cool, efficient, and make me feel like I am in good hands. The absolute best part of their customer rep services is all of my issues have been quickly resolved on the first call.
